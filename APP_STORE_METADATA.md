# App Name
Paddles Up

# Subtitle
Find Courts. Play More.

# Description
Paddles Up helps you spend less time searching and more time playing. Discover pickleball courts nearby, explore court details, and quickly choose where to play based on location, amenities, and current activity.

See what is happening before you leave home. Live availability updates let you check whether courts are open, active, or crowded so you can make smarter decisions and avoid wasted trips.

Pickleball is better with people, and Paddles Up makes it easier to connect. Find other players, coordinate sessions, and build your local community for casual games, drills, or competitive runs.

Track your progress over time with match history and challenges. Log results, look back at recent play, and stay motivated with friendly competition that keeps you improving every time you hit the court.
