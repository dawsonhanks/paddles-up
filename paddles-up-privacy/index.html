Paddles Up

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Paddles Up is a free mobile app for pickleball players — find courts, see what’s happening, connect with friends, and share the game. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it respectful of your privacy.

Information we collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may collect the following:

  • Account & profile — When you create or edit a profile, we store your display name, optional username, optional contact information (such as email or phone, if you choose to provide it), optional profile photo, and related profile fields you enter in the app.
  • Location when the app is open — We request access to your device’s location while you are using Paddles Up so we can show courts near you, sort results by distance, and support features that depend on where you are. We only use foreground location — not continuous background tracking. Exact uses include verifying check-ins near a court.
  • Court check-ins (with GPS verification) — When you check in at a court, we record the check-in, which court it applies to, and timing, and may use your location at that moment to help confirm you are at or near that venue, consistent with how the feature works in the app.
  • Match records — When you log match results (for example wins and losses), we store those records as part of your play history in the app.
  • Friend connections — If you connect with other players in the app, we store those relationships so you can see friends and interact with them.
  • In-app messages — Messages you send and receive through Paddles Up are stored so conversations work reliably and can be shown in your inbox and threads.
  • Court photos — If you upload photos associated with courts, we store those images and link them to the court (and your account) as part of the feature.
  • Court ratings & written reviews — Star ratings, written reviews, and related metadata you submit for courts are stored and may be shown to other users.
  • Game posts & scheduled sessions — When you post games, availability, or scheduled sessions, we store the details you provide (time, place, skill level, notes, and similar fields) so others can discover and join.
  • Push notification tokens — If you opt in to notifications, we store a device token from your phone’s push service so we can deliver alerts you’ve asked for (for example about games, messages, or activity). You can turn these off in your device settings.
  • Favorites & preferences — We save courts you favorite and similar in-app preferences so the experience feels personal and consistent across sessions.
  • Technical & operational data — Like most apps, we process basic technical information needed to run the service (for example app errors, security signals, and identifiers tied to your account session). This helps us keep Paddles Up reliable and secure.

What we don’t do with your data

Some things are simple, so we’ll say them plainly:

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use your data to run third-party advertising or to build an advertising profile about you.
  • We do not track your location when the app isn’t open — location is used in line with your permission and only while you’re actively using Paddles Up (foreground), as described above.

How we use your information

We use what we collect to provide and improve Paddles Up — for example to:

  • Show courts, maps, and activity near you
  • Power check-ins, availability, and community-sourced court information
  • Display your profile and optional public username to other players, when you use those features
  • Let you message friends, post games, and schedule sessions
  • Show ratings, reviews, and photos that you and others contribute
  • Send push notifications you’ve opted into
  • Keep the app secure, fix bugs, and understand aggregate usage so we can make the product better

How we share information

We don’t sell your data. We share information only in these kinds of situations:

  • Service providers — We use trusted vendors to host data, send notifications, and operate the app (for example our database and infrastructure). They process information on our behalf under agreements that protect your privacy.
  • Other users — Content you choose to make social is visible as designed (for example public profile fields, game posts, messages to people you write to, reviews, and photos you attach to courts).
  • Legal & safety — We may disclose information if we believe in good faith it’s required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users, Paddles Up, or the public.

Data storage & retention

Your information is stored securely with our cloud providers. Some items (like live availability or short-lived activity) may expire or roll off quickly; other data (like your profile or messages) is kept while your account uses the app and as needed for legitimate business purposes such as security, backups, and legal compliance. If you have questions about deleting specific data, contact us — we’re happy to help.

Third party services

Paddles Up relies on services that have their own privacy policies, including:

  • Supabase — Database, authentication, and related backend. Privacy Policy
  • Apple Maps — Map display on Apple platforms. Privacy Policy
  • Expo — App framework and tooling. Privacy Policy
  • Push notification platforms — Apple and Google deliver notifications using device tokens according to their own policies.

Children’s privacy

Paddles Up is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate steps.

Your choices & rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete certain personal information, or to object to or restrict some processing. You can update much of your profile directly in the app. For other requests, email us — we’ll work with you in line with applicable law.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we’ll change the date at the top of this page. If the changes are important, we may also notify you through the app or other reasonable means. Continued use of Paddles Up after updates means you accept the revised policy.
